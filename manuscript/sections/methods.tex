\section{Methods}

\subsection{Network construction}

Protein--protein interactions were obtained from STRING v12.0 \citep{Szklarczyk2023}, retaining edges with confidence $\geq$900.
Nodes were restricted to genes with liver expression $\geq$1 TPM (GTEx v8) \citep{GTEx2020}.
The largest connected component was extracted.
Final network: 12,847 nodes, 89,432 edges.

\subsection{Compound targets}

Drug--target interactions were obtained from STITCH v5.0 (confidence $\geq$0.7) \citep{Kuhn2014} and ChEMBL v31 (activity $\leq$10 $\mu$M) \citep{Mendez2019}.
Targets were mapped to gene symbols via UniProt \citep{UniProt2023} and filtered to those present in the liver LCC.
Final: Hyperforin = 10, Quercetin = 62.

\subsection{DILI genes}

Hepatotoxicity genes were obtained from DILIrank \citep{Chen2016}.
Genes absent from the liver LCC were excluded.
Final: 82 genes.

\subsection{Shortest-path proximity (descriptive)}

Mean shortest-path distance:
\begin{equation}
    d(T, D) = \frac{1}{|T| \times |D|} \sum_{t \in T} \sum_{d \in D} \text{dist}(t, d)
\end{equation}
Null distribution: 1,000 degree-matched random target sets (within 25\% degree).
Z-score: $(d_{\text{obs}} - \mu_{\text{null}}) / \sigma_{\text{null}}$.
Negative values indicate closer-than-random proximity.
Proximity is descriptive; it does not test influence.
Seed: 42.

\subsection{Random walk with restart (inferential)}

Influence propagation via RWR \citep{Kohler2008}:
\begin{equation}
    \mathbf{p} = (1 - \alpha) \mathbf{W} \mathbf{p} + \alpha \mathbf{p}_0
\end{equation}
$\alpha = 0.15$ \citep{Guney2016}; $\mathbf{p}_0$ uniform over compound targets.
Convergence: $||\mathbf{p}^{(k+1)} - \mathbf{p}^{(k)}||_1 < 10^{-6}$, max 100 iterations.
Total DILI influence: $I = \sum_{d \in D} p(d)$.
Statistical testing: 1,000 degree-matched permutations, one-tailed.
Seed: 42.

\subsection{Expression-weighted RWR (constraining)}

Adjacency weighted by source-node expression:
\begin{equation}
    A'_{ij} = A_{ij} \times \log_2(\text{TPM}_i + 1)
\end{equation}
Column-normalized.
All other parameters identical.
EWI tests robustness; it is not a separate hypothesis.
Seed: 42.

\subsection{Per-target normalized influence (descriptive)}

\begin{equation}
    \text{PTNI} = \frac{I}{|T|}
\end{equation}
Derived metric; no permutation test.

\subsection{Bootstrap sensitivity}

100 random 10-target samples from Quercetin's pool, scored by RWR.
Seed: 42.

\subsection{Chemical similarity}

Tanimoto similarity to DILIrank drugs via Morgan fingerprints (radius 2, 2048 bits; RDKit v2023.03) \citep{RDKit2023}.
Analog threshold: 0.4 \citep{Maggiora2014}.

\subsection{Statistical analysis}

1,000 permutations; degree-matched within 25\%.
$P$-values at floor reported as $p < 10^{-16}$ for display.
FDR correction (Benjamini--Hochberg) applied where applicable.
Python 3.10, NetworkX 3.1; R 4.3, igraph 1.5.
